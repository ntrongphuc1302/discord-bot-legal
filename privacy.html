# Privacy Policy _Last updated: June 21, 2026_ Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data **PXZero** (the "Bot") accesses, collects, and how it is used. ## 1. Data We Access and Collect The Bot interacts with data provided via the Discord API. Depending on the commands you use, this may include: - **User Identifiers:** Discord User IDs and Usernames. - **Server Identifiers:** Server (Guild) IDs, Channel IDs, and Role IDs. - **Command Content:** The content of commands you explicitly send to the Bot to process your requests. _Note: The Bot does not log, read, or store your private personal conversations or messages unrelated to the Bot's specific commands._ ## 2. How We Use the Data Any data accessed or temporarily processed is used solely to: - Execute and respond to the commands you trigger. - Maintain basic functionalities of the Bot within your server. - Ensure the security and stability of the Bot (e.g., rate-limiting to prevent spam). ## 3. Data Storage and Sharing - **Storage:** Data is only stored temporarily in memory or config files required for the Bot to function. We do not maintain a permanent database of your personal information. - **Sharing:** We **NEVER** sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. ## 4. Data Deletion Since data is processed in real-time and minimized, we generally do not hold persistent logs. However, if you want to ensure any cached data related to your server or user ID is completely removed, simply kick the Bot from your server or contact the developer. ## 5. Agreement By using the Bot, you consent to the collection and use of information as outlined in this policy. If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will be revised.
